Is the signature legally binding and eIDAS compliant? Yes. The service utilizes Strong Electronic Identification (Finnish Trust Network) to create Advanced Electronic Signatures (AES) compliant with EU eIDAS Regulation Article 26. The final PDF is sealed with a digital certificate, ensuring integrity. Note: While AES is the standard for most commercial contracts in the Nordics, the service does not currently issue Qualified Electronic Signatures (QES).
How is personal data handled (GDPR)? Unlike many consumer services, we operate strictly as the Data Processor acting on your instructions. You remain the Data Controller and retain full ownership of your customer data. We do not use your contract data for our own purposes.
Can the signatures be verified independently? Yes. We do not lock you in. All signed documents follow the PAdES (PDF Advanced Electronic Signature) standard. You can verify the validity of the signature, the digital seal, and the timestamp offline using standard software like Adobe Acrobat Reader, even if you stop using our service.

Is this a long-term archiving solution? No. Allekirjoitus.fi is a signing pipeline, not a permanent archive. By default, documents are retained for 90 days (configurable up to 365 days for Enterprise) to allow for download.

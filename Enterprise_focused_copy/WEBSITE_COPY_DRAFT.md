### WHAT IS ALLEKIRJOITUS.FI (Service Explainer - immediately below hero)

**Section Heading:**
Sign Contracts Digitally. Verify Identity Securely.

**Lead Paragraph:**
Allekirjoitus.fi is Finland's enterprise e-signature platform. Send documents for signing, recipients authenticate with their bank credentials, and you get legally binding contracts back—signed, sealed, and independently verifiable.

**Three-Column Quick Value:**

| Column 1 | Column 2 | Column 3 |
|----------|----------|----------|
| **Send** | **Sign** | **Done** |
| Upload your contract, add signers | They verify identity with Finnish Bank ID or Mobile ID | Receive tamper-proof PDF with embedded certificate |

**Key Points (below columns):**
- No accounts needed for signers—they use credentials they already have
- EU eIDAS Advanced Electronic Signature standard
- Works in Adobe Reader offline—no vendor lock-in

### SIGNING METHODS

**Section Heading:**
Signing Methods

#### AES Card (Primary)
**Badge:** Standard
**Title:** Advanced Electronic Signature
**Description:** EU eIDAS compliant signatures with strong identification. The default for legally binding agreements.
**Methods:** Finnish Bank IDs, Mobile ID, Nordic-Baltic eIDs
**Use Cases:** Commercial contracts, employment agreements, loan documents, public sector
**Price:** €0.80/signatory

#### SES Card (Secondary)
**Badge:** Flexible
**Title:** Simple Electronic Signature
**Description:** Agile signing for lower-risk scenarios when strong authentication is not required.
**Methods:** SMS OTP, Email OTP, Touch signature (InkSign)
**Use Cases:** Delivery confirmations, internal approvals, B2C acknowledgments
**Price:** €0.65/signatory
**Note:** Enterprise tier only
